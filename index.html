Our Story

1976 — The Journey Begins

One pump. One chair. One big dream. That's how it started in 1976, on the dusty stretch of NH60 in Pahala, Bhubaneswar. No app. No GPS. Just a man who believed that a petrol pump could be more — a place where people stop, breathe, and leave with a smile.

"We didn't just pump fuel. We built trust. One customer at a time. Over 50 years, that hasn't changed."
— The Founder

Five decades later, Chintamaniswar Filling Station is not just a fuel stop. It's a landmark. A place where generations of travellers, truckers, families, and neighbours have found more than petrol — they've found a second home on the highway.

Through monsoons, heatwaves, cyclones, and economic shifts — the pump has never closed. The trust has never wavered. The smile has never changed.
